Biography

I was born and raised in the East San Francisco Bay Area. I've always had an interest in computers from and early age but never expected to make a career out of it.
I've also really had an interest in competative PC games. When I was younger I really enjoyed playing Everquest, Starcraft, and Day of Defeat. As of late I've been really interested in Counter Strike: Global Offensive and Starcraft 2.
My interest in PC games naturally led me to have interests in PC's themselves. That is, in building them. I have extensive experience in building personal computers and frequently follow the newest and fastest components, usually CPU's and GPU's. I'm really fascinated in how technology evolves.
I'm also really interested in computer security, although I really am a novice. I try to learn as much as I can on my spare time and hope to one day specialize in computer security.

CodeCademy Website

HackerRank

HackerRank is a site specifically for those wanting to practice their programming skills. HackerRank offers hundreds of programming challenges and frequent programming challenge contests where you can compete with many hackers on the site. Solving challenges gives your profile points for which can aid you to increase in rank.
What I've found most helpful about HackerRank is that the challenges recquire you to consider runtime to recieve the maximum number of points for a given challenge—you can't use a bruteforce solution for everything. Thus, the site forces you to always consider the efficiency of your programs—which is generally good practice and a soughtout skill in the software industry.
HackerRank Website
